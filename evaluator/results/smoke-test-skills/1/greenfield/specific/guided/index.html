Our Philosophy

We believe coffee is more than a beverage; it's a ritual. Our beans are sourced directly from micro-lot farmers in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Sumatra, ensuring fair wages and exceptional quality.

Every batch is roasted in small quantities to highlight the unique terroir of its origin.

Signature Blend Ingredients

  • Arabica Beans: 100% Ethiopian Yirgacheffe
  • Notes: Jasmine, Lemon, Bergamot
  • Roast: Medium-to-Light
  • Origin: Gedeo Zone, Southern Ethiopia
