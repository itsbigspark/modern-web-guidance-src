Our Story

Passion for coffee since 2010.

The Daily Grind started with a simple idea: that coffee should be more than just a morning ritual—it should be an experience. Founded in 2010 by two friends in a small garage, we've grown into a beloved community hub for coffee lovers.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms across the globe, ensuring fair wages for farmers and the highest quality beans for you. Every batch is roasted locally in small batches to highlight the unique flavor profiles of each origin.

But we're more than just coffee. We're a place to gather, to work, to relax, and to connect. Whether you're grabbing a quick espresso on your way to work or settling in for an afternoon of reading, we're here to make your day a little better.
