Prioritized Preloading

Orchestrate background work using scheduler.postTask. Compare scheduling with default priority vs "background" priority to allow high-priority interactions to jump the queue.

Simulating Prioritized Preloading:

  1. Click 2. Trigger Urgent Task to confirm it runs immediately.
  2. Click Clear Log.
  3. Click 1a. Preload (Default Priority) and then IMMEDIATELY click 2. Trigger Urgent Task.
    👉 Check Log: The Urgent Task is blocked and runs after all Preload items finish.
  4. Click Clear Log.
  5. Click 1b. Preload (Background Priority) and then IMMEDIATELY click 2. Trigger Urgent Task.
    👉 Check Log: The Urgent Task jumps the queue and runs before the remaining background items.
